PRIVACY POLICY

At our restaurant, we understand that privacy is important to our customers. We are committed to protecting your personal information and providing transparency about how we collect, use, and share your information when you visit our website. This privacy policy outlines our practices regarding your personal information and how we keep it secure.

Information We Collect

When you visit our website, we may collect personal and usage information. Personal information includes your name, email address, and phone number if you fill out a contact form or make a reservation through our website. Usage information includes your IP address, browser type, operating system, and the pages you visit on our website, which we collect through cookies and other tracking technologies.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information for several purposes, including to process and respond to your requests such as when you make a reservation or contact us through our website. We also use your information to personalize your experience, such as by remembering your preferences or tailoring content and advertisements to your interests. Additionally, we use your information to improve our website, such as by analyzing usage patterns and making improvements to the website. Finally, we may use your information to comply with legal obligations such as responding to legal requests or preventing fraudulent activity.

How We Share Your Information

We may share your information with third parties in several circumstances. First, we may share your information with third-party service providers who help us to operate our website, such as website hosting or analytics providers. We may also share your information with business partners who help us to offer our services, such as payment processors or marketing partners. Additionally, we may share your information with law enforcement or other government entities if required by law or if we believe in good faith that such disclosure is necessary to protect our rights, your safety, or the safety of others. Finally, we may share your information in connection with a merger, acquisition, or other business transaction.

Your Rights and Choices

You have certain rights and choices regarding your personal information. First, you have the right to request access to the personal information we hold about you. You also have the right to request that we correct any inaccurate or incomplete personal information we hold about you. Additionally, you have the right to request that we delete your personal information, subject to certain exceptions. You may also object to the processing of your personal information or withdraw your consent if we are processing your personal information based on your consent.

Security

We take appropriate measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. We use secure encryption protocols to transmit sensitive information such as credit card numbers, and we store personal information on secure servers.

However, no system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee the security of your personal information. If you have reason to believe that your interaction with us is no longer secure, please notify us immediately using the contact information provided below.

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We may use cookies and other tracking technologies to collect usage information about our website. Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you access our website. We use cookies to remember your preferences and to analyze usage patterns to improve our website.

You can choose to disable cookies through your browser settings, although this may affect your ability to use certain features of our website.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time by posting a new version on our website. We encourage you to review this policy periodically for any changes.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about our privacy policy, please contact us using the contact information provided on our website. We will be happy to address any concerns or questions you may have.

Thank you for visiting our website and for entrusting us with your personal information. We take your privacy seriously and will continue to work hard to protect your information and maintain your trust.

Queries related to Privacy Policy

If you have a query, concern, or complaint in relation to collection or usage of your personal information under this Privacy Policy, please contact us at privacy.grievance@khaao.com.
